Beyond Competition

Outreach at Nyx

FTC Team Nyx 34612 believes robotics is bigger than the field. Our outreach program is about sharing STEM, mentoring younger students, connecting with our community, and making engineering feel exciting, accessible, and real.

From demonstrations and workshops to mentorship and volunteering, we use outreach to inspire future builders and show how robotics can create lasting impact beyond a single season.
